Lando's Ultimate Firestick Setup Guide

Step 1: Preparation

- Open the Settings app on Firestick
- Navigate to "Device"
- Choose "Developer Options"
- Choose "Apps from Unkown Sources"
- Choose "Turn On"

Step 2: Downloader

- Hold the Mic button on remote and say "Downloader"
- Choose the Downloader app
- Install Downloader
- Open Downloader app

Step 3: FileLinked

- From the Home tab in Downloader app, type the following link: http://troypoint.com/FL
- Choose "Go"
- Choose "Install"
After install, choose "Done" (DO NOT OPEN)
- Choose "Delete" (install file is no longer needed. Saves space on your Firestick)
- Hold Home button on remote
- Choose "Apps"
- Navigate to FileLinked app and open it.
- WARNING: If you receive a connection error, unplug Firestick from wall and plug back in.

Step 4: Downloading Apps

- Once FileLinked is open, enter the following code: 73570033
- Choose "Continue"
- Read the onscreen instructions
- You will need to choose each app individually to download, and then again to install.
- You will now have all of the current apps downloaded on your Firestick.
- WARNING: Make sure to follow Step 5 to set up Kodi Xanax!

Step 5: Kodi Xanax Setup

- Open the Xanax application
- Wait for pop-up, choose "Continue"
- Choose "Build Menu"
- Choose "[18.1] Xanax 18 Host 3 (v1.2)
- Choose "(Xanax) Fresh Install"
- Choose "Continue"
- After download completes, choose "Force Close"
- WARNING: If app does not force close, unplug Firestick from the wall for 15 seconds and plug back in
- You can now open and use Kodi Xanax, enjoy!
